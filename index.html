About Anvira

The studio behind the dashboards

Anvira is a data & business intelligence consultancy founded by Anushka Bhattarai. We partner with small businesses, startups, NGOs, and e-commerce teams to turn raw, scattered data into clear dashboards, automated reports, and decisions they can trust.

With deep, hands-on expertise across Power BI, Tableau, SQL, Python, and Excel — backed by a First Class Honours degree in Computer Science and certifications from IBM, UC Davis, and Google — we bring enterprise-grade analytics to teams that don't have an in-house data department. Every project is owned end to end, so you get one accountable partner who cares less about the chart and more about the decision it helps you make.
