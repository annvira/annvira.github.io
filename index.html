01About Me

A data & BI consultant who turns scattered, confusing data into clear dashboards, automated reports, and decisions you can act on.

Most businesses don't have a data problem — they have a clarity problem. The numbers are there, buried across spreadsheets and tools, but no one has the time or the system to make sense of them. That's the gap I close.

I'm a data & business intelligence consultant with a First Class Honours degree in Computer Science and hands-on expertise across Power BI, Tableau, SQL, Python, and Excel. Whether you're a startup tracking your first KPIs, an NGO reporting impact, or an e-commerce business chasing growth, I'll help you put your data to work.

05How It Works

A simple, transparent path from data to decisions

No mystery, no jargon. Here's exactly how we'll work together.

Step 01

Discover

A free call to understand your business, your goals, and the decisions you're trying to make — before touching a single dataset.

Step 02

Audit & plan

I review your current data, tools, and reports, then map out what I'll build and the timeline. You approve the plan before we begin.

Step 03

Build

I clean and model your data, then design dashboards and reports around your KPIs — sharing progress along the way, so there are no surprises.

Step 04

Deliver & train

You get a polished, working dashboard plus a walkthrough so your team can actually use it. Clear documentation included.

Step 05

Support & improve

Your business evolves and so should your reporting. I offer ongoing support to refine metrics, add views, and keep everything running.
