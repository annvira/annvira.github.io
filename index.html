01About Me

I help businesses see clearly and decide confidently

A data & BI consultant who turns scattered, confusing data into clear dashboards, automated reports, and decisions you can act on.

Most businesses don't have a data problem — they have a clarity problem. The numbers are there, buried across spreadsheets and tools, but no one has the time or the system to make sense of them. That's the gap I close.

I'm a data & business intelligence consultant with a First Class Honours degree in Computer Science and hands-on expertise across Power BI, Tableau, SQL, Python, and Excel. I design dashboards, build BI systems, and automate the reporting that quietly drains your team's time — always with one goal: helping you make smarter, faster, evidence-based decisions.

Because I own each project end to end, you get a single accountable partner who cares less about the chart and more about the decision it helps you make. Whether you're a startup tracking your first KPIs, an NGO reporting impact, or an e-commerce business chasing growth, I'll help you put your data to work.
